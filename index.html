Data Collection

In order to assess the various factors that may influence air quality in urban areas across the United State, 4 main data sources were required:

  1. Air Quality Data - NO2, O3, SO2, CO, PM2.5, and PM10
  2. Historical Weather Data - Precip, Wind, Temp, Atmospheric Pressure
  3. Demographic Data - Population Density, Area, Population, Metropolitan vs. Micropolitan Area
  4. Transit Data - Modes of transportation
